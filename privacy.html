01

Introduction

At Kadongo Cool Breez Hotel Resort, we are committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our guests, website visitors, and business partners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal data when you interact with our services.

By making a reservation by telephone, visiting our website, or staying at our property on Mfangano Island, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of our services.

Our Commitment: We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is used solely to enhance your stay experience and manage our resort operations.

02

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you make a telephone reservation or stay with us, we may collect:

  • Full name and contact telephone number
  • Email address (if provided)
  • National identification or passport details (for guest registration as required by Kenyan law)
  • Check-in and check-out dates
  • Number of guests and room preferences
  • Special requests (dietary requirements, accessibility needs, celebrations)

Website Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring website (if applicable)

What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as racial origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, health data, or biometric information unless voluntarily provided for specific service needs (e.g., medical assistance requests during your stay).

03

How We Use Your Information

Your information enables us to:

  • Process Reservations: Confirm and manage your telephone bookings, check-in, and check-out procedures.
  • Personalize Your Stay: Anticipate your preferences and provide tailored recommendations for your time on Mfangano Island.
  • Communicate with You: Send booking confirmations, pre-arrival details, and post-stay follow-ups.
  • Improve Our Services: Analyze guest feedback and website usage to enhance our offerings.
  • Comply with Legal Obligations: Meet Kenyan tourism regulations, tax requirements, and guest registration laws.
  • Ensure Security: Protect the safety of our property, guests, and staff.
info No Marketing Without Consent

We will never use your personal information for unsolicited marketing communications. If we wish to send you occasional updates about special offers or cultural events, we will ask for your explicit consent first.

04

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist with bookings, communications, or website hosting (bound by confidentiality agreements).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by Kenyan law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (guests will be notified).
  • With Your Consent: For specific purposes you explicitly authorize.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes under any circumstances.

05

Your Rights & Choices

Under the Kenyan Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Restriction: Object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for any processing based on consent at any time.
  • Complain: Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) of Kenya.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided at the end of this policy. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

06

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

  • Secure storage of guest registration records in locked facilities
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Staff training on data protection best practices
  • Regular review of our security procedures
  • Secure handling of any payment information (processed via trusted providers)

Important: While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. If you suspect a data breach, please notify us immediately.

07

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies to ensure basic functionality and improve your browsing experience. We use only essential cookies that:

  • Remember your preferences during your visit
  • Enable basic website functionality
  • Help us understand how visitors use our site (via anonymized analytics)

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party marketing cookies. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may limit website functionality.

For more details, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

08

Children's Privacy

Kadongo Cool Breez Hotel Resort welcomes families. When children stay with us, we collect only the information necessary for their safety and comfort (name, age, and any special needs), provided by their parent or guardian.

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child without consent, please contact us immediately.

09

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including:

  • Guest Records: Retained for the duration of your stay plus 5 years for legal and tax compliance.
  • Website Analytics: Anonymized data retained for up to 26 months.
  • Communication Records: Retained for 3 years after last contact.

After these periods, your data is securely deleted or anonymized, unless longer retention is required by law.

10

International Transfers

Kadongo Cool Breez Hotel Resort is based in Kenya. Your personal information is primarily processed and stored in Kenya. If you are visiting from outside Kenya, your information may be transferred to and processed in Kenya, which has data protection laws under the Data Protection Act, 2019.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international data transfers, in compliance with applicable data protection regulations.

11

Modifications to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or operational needs. Changes take effect immediately upon posting to our website.

We encourage periodic review of this page. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12

Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of Kenya, including the Data Protection Act, 2019.

Any disputes arising from this policy shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Kenya. We encourage good-faith resolution through direct communication before pursuing legal action.
