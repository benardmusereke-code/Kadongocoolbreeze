Reservations & Booking

Telephone Reservations

All reservations at Kadongo Cool Breez Hotel Resort are handled directly by our management team via telephone. This ensures every guest receives personalised assistance, accurate availability information, and tailored recommendations for their stay.

Primary Line: +254 756 585 016
Alternative Line: +254 715 249 204

Booking Confirmation

A reservation is only confirmed once our management team has verbally acknowledged availability and agreed to hold the requested room for your specified dates. Email confirmation may follow for your records.

Information Required at Booking

When making your reservation, please be prepared to provide:

  • Full name and contact telephone number
  • Preferred check-in and check-out dates
  • Number of guests (adults and children)
  • Preferred room category (Single, Standard Double, Deluxe, or Family Suite)
  • Any special requests or requirements (dietary, accessibility, celebrations)

Availability

All reservations are subject to room availability at the time of booking. We recommend booking in advance, especially during peak seasons (December–March and June–October), to secure your preferred dates and room type.

Payments & Pricing

All prices are quoted in Kenyan Shillings (KES) and are subject to seasonal adjustments. Current rates will be confirmed at the time of your telephone reservation.

Payment Methods

We accept payment via M-Pesa, bank transfer, and cash upon arrival. Payment details will be provided by our management team at the time of booking.

Price Accuracy

We strive to maintain accurate pricing on our website. However, we reserve the right to correct any pricing errors. If a significant discrepancy occurs, our team will contact you before confirming your reservation.

Current Indicative Rates: Single Room from KES 2,000/night • Standard Double from KES 3,500/night • Deluxe Room from KES 5,000/night • Family Suite from KES 7,500/night. All rates subject to seasonal adjustment.

  • Standard Check-out: 11:00 AM

If you anticipate arriving outside standard check-in hours, please inform our management team in advance so we can accommodate your arrival comfortably.

06

Children Policy

Families are warmly welcome at Kadongo Cool Breez Hotel Resort. Children may be accommodated under clearly observed house rules and safety guidelines to ensure a comfortable and peaceful stay for all guests.

  • Children under the age of 12 must be supervised by a parent or guardian at all times within the resort premises.
  • Children are not permitted in the bar area or in dining spaces during late evening service.
  • Parents and guardians are responsible for the behaviour and safety of their children throughout their stay.
  • Additional bedding or cots for children may be requested at the time of booking, subject to availability.
07

Property Rules

To ensure the comfort and safety of all guests, the following rules apply:

  • Visitors: Non-resident visitors are welcome in public areas during daylight hours (8:00 AM – 8:00 PM) with prior notice to reception. Overnight visitors are not permitted without prior arrangement.
  • Security: Our property is monitored by professional security personnel operating day and night. A nearby police station provides additional security for the area.
  • Valuables: Guests are advised to keep personal valuables secure. The resort is not responsible for loss or damage to personal belongings left in rooms or public areas.
  • Pets: Pets are not permitted on the property, with the exception of certified service animals arranged in advance.
  • Alcohol: Alcoholic beverages may only be consumed in designated areas (restaurant, bar, and private rooms). Guests are expected to consume responsibly.
  • Illegal substances: The possession or use of illegal substances on the property is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate eviction and reporting to authorities.
08

Smoking Policy

Kadongo Cool Breez Hotel Resort is a non-smoking property in all indoor areas, including guest rooms, dining areas, and common spaces.

  • Smoking is permitted only in designated outdoor areas.
  • Electronic cigarettes and vaping devices are subject to the same restrictions.
  • A cleaning fee of KES 5,000 will be charged for smoking in non-designated areas.
09

Noise & Quiet Hours

We are committed to providing a peaceful environment for all our guests. To respect the comfort of everyone staying with us, we observe quiet hours between 10:00 PM and 7:00 AM daily.

During quiet hours, guests are requested to keep noise to a minimum in rooms and outdoor areas. Excessive noise or disruptive behaviour at any time may result in a request to leave the property without refund.

Seasonal traditional music performances are scheduled in advance and announced to guests. These cultural celebrations are an important part of our heritage and are conducted with consideration for all guests.

10

Cancellation Policy

Since all bookings are made by telephone, cancellations must also be made by telephone to ensure proper confirmation.

Standard Cancellation

  • Cancellations made 7 or more days before the scheduled check-in date: No charge.
  • Cancellations made 3 to 6 days before check-in: 50% of the first night's rate will be charged.
  • Cancellations made less than 3 days before check-in or no-shows: 100% of the first night's rate will be charged.

How to Cancel

To cancel your reservation, please call us directly at +254 756 585 016 or +254 715 249 204. You will receive verbal confirmation of the cancellation and any applicable charges.

Force Majeure

In the event of circumstances beyond our control (natural disasters, ferry cancellations, government restrictions), we will work with you to reschedule your stay or provide a full refund, at your preference.

We understand that plans can change. We encourage guests to communicate with us as early as possible so we can assist with rebooking or cancellation in the most accommodating way.
